about me

Hi All, I'm Harish Rathod!, and my Stage name is "Townsinger" Music Composer, and Lyric Writer who sings Hindi Devotional Songs, Does stage performances, sings Melody Songs, etc. I have also created many videos on Youtube and other social media platforms, which have gained popularity in India. I have been singing for the past few years and released my music album, which consists of many melodious songs. I have also worked with many other singers as a lyricist. My album Mujhko Tere Darsham Hojate was released on YouTube, received more than 250K views within a short period, and still receives thousands daily. My other album Khatu Dham Aaya has also received a significant response from all over the world with more than 190K views, and the recent album Main Saans Hoon received more than 300k views and continues to rock!
